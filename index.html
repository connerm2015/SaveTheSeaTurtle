Save the Sea Turtle

Check out the newest web-based game called “ Save the Sea Turtle!”

Keep Squirt safe on his adventure! Help him catch his favorite snacks, fish and jellyfish while avoiding obstacles that Sea Turtles face on a daily basis. All proceeds from the game will go to Sea Turtle Foundation. They help protect this endangered species through research, education and action.
