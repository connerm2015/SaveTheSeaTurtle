Save the Sea Turtle

About the Game

The purpose of creating "Save the Sea Turtle" is to bring awareness to human interaction with the endangered species. Globally it's estimated that approximately 52% of all sea turtles have eaten plastic. Also, 91% of turtles entangled in discarded fishing gear died. These are all preventable statistics and our game hopes to further research for sea turtles by giving all proceeds to the Sea Turtle Foundation.
